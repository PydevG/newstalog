Content Guidelines

1. Overview

Stalog is a platform for sharing valuable and respectful content. To maintain a positive environment, we have established the following content guidelines.

2. Prohibited Content

Users must not post:

  • Hate speech, discrimination, or any form of harassment.
  • False or misleading information.
  • Explicit, violent, or inappropriate content.
  • Spam, self-promotion, or irrelevant links.
  • Copyrighted material without permission.

3. Content Ownership

By posting on Stalog, you confirm that:

  • You own the content or have permission to post it.
  • You grant StaBlog a non-exclusive license to display and distribute your content.

4. Moderation & Reporting

We reserve the right to remove content that violates these guidelines. Users can report inappropriate posts for review.

5. Consequences of Violations

Failure to follow these guidelines may result in:

  • Content removal.
  • Warnings or temporary account suspension.
  • Permanent ban for severe violations.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these guidelines, please contact us.
